Our Story

Avenues of the Caribbean (AOC) is a drop-off Caribbean catering service in Washington, D.C. Our mission is to serve the richness and versatility of the Caribbean through a carefully curated menu of authentic and fusion dishes with a strong Haitian influence.

From griot to curry shrimp, black mushroom rice to rasta pasta — every dish is made to transport you. Let us feed you and be your Avenue to the Caribbean.
